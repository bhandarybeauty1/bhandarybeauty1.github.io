About

Hi I'm Eva! I am a makeup artist based in Washington, D.C. and I take passion in spreading glam through our nation's capital.
In fifth grade, I was introduced to the world of glamour. I started off learning the basics from my mom, watching endless tutorials and practicing on all my friends. By college, my hardwork had paid off, I was hired as a Glammie at the renowned bridal studio, Lou Stevens Glam Squad.
When I'm not reaching towards my brushes, I'm usually singing along in the passenger seat enjoying spontaneous road trips or finding the best places to eat wherever I go!
